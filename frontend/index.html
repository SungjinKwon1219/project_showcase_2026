Features

Built for real people,
not textbooks

Every body is different. Safer adapts its model to you over time using Bayesian shrinkage estimation, getting smarter with every session you log.

🧬
Personalized r Estimation
We replace the Widmark formula's fixed body water constant with a regression model trained on NHANES data. Your r adapts as you log sessions.
📊
Real-Time BAC Curve
Visualize your BAC trajectory over time — before, during, and after drinking — with live-updating estimates as conditions change.
🌙
Context-Aware Modifiers
Slept 4 hours? Skipped dinner? Dehydrated? Safer adjusts your elimination rate β using pre-session inputs that actually matter.
🔮
Bayesian Profile Update
As sessions accumulate, population priors fade. Your personal history carries more weight. The model becomes uniquely yours.
📝
Session History
Log every drink, every feeling, every context. Your history feeds the model and lets you track patterns across time.
🛡️
Safer, Not Preachy
This isn't a sobriety app. It's a harm reduction tool. We give you honest information so you can make smart, informed choices.

How It Works

Four steps to safer nights

01
Build your profile
Enter your biological data — sex, weight, age — to initialize your personal r estimate from our NHANES-trained model.
02
Set the context
Tell Safer about today: how much you slept, when you ate, how hydrated you are. These adjust your β elimination rate.
03
Log your drinks
Track drinks in real-time as the night goes on. Your BAC curve updates live with each entry.
04
Model learns you
Post-session feedback refines your personal estimates. The more you log, the more accurate Safer becomes for you.
